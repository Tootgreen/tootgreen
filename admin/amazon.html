How to add an Amazon URL:
  1. Find the product on amazon.co.uk and copy the URL
  2. Paste it into the box next to the matching product — it must contain /dp/
  3. Once done, click Copy JSON and paste it into amazon-urls.json in the repo, then push to Vercel
